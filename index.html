About Me

I'm Kingston Wereloo Leyiibe, an Environmental Sustainability Specialist, Climate Educator, and the Founder of Greenable Initiative Africa, a youth-led NGO driving grassroots climate action and education across Nigeria. With a background in Environmental Biotechnology and over three years of hands-on experience, I’ve led community-centered projects focused on environmental awareness, waste recovery, biogas production, and sustainability training. My work bridges science, policy, and public engagement, turning complex climate issues into practical, people-driven solutions.
