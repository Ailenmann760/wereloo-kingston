About Me

I'm Kingston Wereloo Leyiibe, an Environmental Sustainability Specialist, Climate Educator, and the Founder of Greenable Initiative Africa, a youth-led NGO driving grassroots climate action and education across Nigeria.

With a background in Environmental Biotechnology and over three years of hands-on experience, I’ve led community-centered projects focused on environmental awareness, waste recovery, biogas production, and sustainability training. My work bridges science, policy, and public engagement, turning complex climate issues into practical, people-driven solutions.

I currently serve as the Sustainability Manager at Greenable Initiative Africa. Through this role, I lead strategic initiatives that promote waste recovery, climate literacy, clean energy awareness, and sustainable development in underserved communities. I'm also an active advocate for youth inclusion in climate policy and have been selected for programs such as Youth4Climate, the UN SDSN National Green Campaign, and the World Bank Youth Summit.

I also write and speak on environmental topics, including climate change, circular economy, and Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) principles, simplifying complex ideas to spark public interest and inspire action.

My mission is to empower individuals and institutions to embed sustainability in their choices, policies, and futures.
